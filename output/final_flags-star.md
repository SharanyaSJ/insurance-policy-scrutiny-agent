## Final Verdict

### Status: FAIL

### Reasoning:
1. **CRITICAL deviation:** Sum Insured Grade B reduced by 10% (affects 600 employees, largest segment)
2. **CRITICAL violation:** Organ Donor benefit excluded (mandatory RFP clause)
3. **HIGH deviations:**
   - Day Care restricted to Schedule II (not IRDAI list)
   - Slip-in chronic disease co-pay (10% on claims exceeding ₹1L)
4. **LOW declaration integrity:** Only 1 of 7 deviations declared in Quote (14.3%)

### Positive Aspects:
- Network hospital coverage exceeds RFP requirements (11,000 vs 5,000)
- Claim settlement ratio good (94.6%)
- Premium competitive (₹2.21 Cr for 4,200 lives)

### Recommendation:
**REJECT POLICY** and request revised quotation addressing:
- Flag #1: Restore Grade B SI to ₹5,00,000
- Flag #2: Include Organ Donor benefit of ₹1,00,000
- Flag #3: Confirm Day Care Schedule II = IRDAI list or expand coverage
- Flag #4: Remove chronic disease co-pay (not in RFP)
